About Us

Basiea Electrifying Solutions is a proudly South African Electrical Services Company, committed to delivering innovative, reliable, and affordable energy solutions. We specialise in residential, commercial, and renewable energy installations, with a growing focus on sustainable technologies like solar.

Our mission is to empower communities with clean, safe, and future-forward electrification. Whether it's wiring a new home or installing an off-grid solar PV system, we bring professional service and technical excellence to every project.

Our Services

Residential Electrical Installations

Safe and compliant wiring and installations for new homes and upgrades.

Solar Installations

Standalone or hybrid solar systems designed for energy efficiency and independence.

Backup Power Solutions

Energy Audits & Consulting

Maintenance & Repairs
